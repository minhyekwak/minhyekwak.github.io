Measure participantsâ€™ brain wave : The installation will move according to their alpha and gamma waves.
Simulation of Dream status : alpha and gamma wave express the concept of dream. Gamma wave actually sparks when people get insights, idea, or realization called "Aha! Moment." According to neuroscientific research, people who are in vivid dreams have gamma wave. The noticeable fact is this gamma wave is preceded by alpha and theta waves which represents relax, and REM sleep. In other words, when we rest, this "Aha! Moment" come.
